# iOS Device Registration for Ad Hoc Distribution
# Format: Device ID<tab>Device Name
# 
# To find your device UDID:
# 1. Connect device to Mac
# 2. Open Finder → Click on device
# 3. Click under device name to show UDID
# 4. Right-click UDID → Copy
#
# Example:
# 00008030-001234567890001E	John's iPhone 14 Pro
# 00008110-000123456789001E	Jane's iPad Air
